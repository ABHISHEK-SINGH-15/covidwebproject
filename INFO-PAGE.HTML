SYMPTOMS



Previous Next



COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.Seek immediate medical attention if you have serious symptoms. Always call before visiting your doctor or health facility.People with mild symptoms who are otherwise healthy should manage their symptoms at home. On average it takes 5–6 days from when someone is infected with the virus for symptoms to show, however it can take up to 14 days.


Most Common Symptoms

    fever
    dry cough
    tiredness
    difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
    chest pain or pressure
    loss of speech or movement

Less Common Symptoms

    aches and pains
    sore throat
    conjunctivitis

HOW IT SPREADS



Previous Next



"COVID-19 is a new disease and we are still learning about how it spreads" according to the US Centers for Disease Control and Prevention.The virus is thought to spread mainly from person-to-person.This idea, that large droplets of virus-laden mucus are the primary mode of transmission, guides the US CDC's advice to maintain at least a 6-foot distance: "Maintaining good social distance (about 6 feet) is very important in preventing the spread of COVID-19.People can catch COVID-19 from others who have the virus. The disease can spread from person to person through small droplets from the nose or mouth which are spread when a person with COVID-19 coughs or exhales. These droplets land on objects and surfaces around the person


PREVENTION



Previous Next



To Prevent The Spread Of COVID-19


    Clean your hands often. Use soap and water, or an alcohol-based hand rub
    Don’t touch your eyes, nose or mouth
    Maintain a safe distance from anyone who is coughing or sneezing
    Stay home if you feel unwell.
    Cover your nose and mouth with your bent elbow or a tissue when you cough or sneeze.
    Follow the directions of your local health authority
    If you have a fever, cough and difficulty breathing, seek medical attention. Call in advance
